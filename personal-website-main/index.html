My Journey

As a young women who transferred her major to Computer Science, I have been going through an amazing process of self-discovery.

Prior to entering the field of Computer Science, I completed my Master's in Project Management in Northwestern University and have a strong management background with expertise in finance and marketing. During my studies, I delved into the world of computational finance tools and discovered how they serve as a crucial foundation for supporting strategic management in today's technology-driven landscape. My internship as a marketing analyst has further reminded me how vastly tech plays the role of game changer in driving innovation and acquiring core competence.

Until then, I realized that I wanted to play an integral role in implementing technology into the finance and project management setting, as well as using technology to create easily accessible resources for clients, which led me to pursue a degree in Computer Science.

Over my journey of exploring programming, I have continuously strived to gain and improve my knowledge throughout all aspects of the tech. In my MSCS program, I have had a deep understanding of algorithm and programmed in multiple front-end and back-end languages.

I consider myself to be a hard working, outgoing individual who is detail-oriented, enthusiastic and willing to learn! Currently, I am ready to embrace job opportunities and looking forward to the challenges of real world work life, whether professionally or mentally.
