About Me

Hi there, you finally found NEMO!

I am currently working as a research assistant at Sichuan University and am seeking for a Ph.D. position in Information or Communication for Fall 2024.

My research interests are centered around human-technology interaction and media psychology , particularly how emerging technologies and media shape users' behavior and how they can be leveraged to enhance well-being, education, and other meaningful outcomes. Specifically, I am fascinated by the realms of online programs, social robots, and virtual reality. I am keen to explore in what ways can we design human's interaction with these technologies to yield beneficial outcomes. Exploring the potential of technologies also explores the potential of myself.
